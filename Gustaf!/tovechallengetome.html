Our mission

Since 1998, The FruitGuys has been on a mission to support American farms and combat hunger. Buying your fruit from The FruitGuys helps our family-owned and operated B Corp business sustain the small independent farmers we support and source from and the hunger-relief non-profits we donate to through our GoodWorks programs.

In 2019, we became B Corp certified in recognition of our consistent values and practices to use business as a force for good.

We donate all visually imperfect and excess fresh fruit to local food banks and charities in the communities we serve. Your business with us helps support our weekly fresh fruit donations to hunger-relief organizations across the country.
