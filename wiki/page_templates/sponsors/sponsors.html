iGEM 2021 Team Impact Grant

We are immensely honored to have been one of the 90 ambitious teams that received the iGEM 2021 Team Impacts Grant. This first-of-its-kind initiative was supported by the Frederick Gardner Cottrell Foundation and provided selected iGEM projects the opportunity to take their project to the next level. Needless to say, the grant has been extremely important to execute our work and we are incredibly grateful that the iGEM association saw the value behind our project.

We were proud to be one of the teams which fit the criteria of this grant. We are proud to be one of the teams which tackles climate change, innovative agricultural solutions and even potential reduction of future global pandemics. You can read more about the iGEM 2021 Team Impact Grant here.
